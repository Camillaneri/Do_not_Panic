    Search by metadata:

    The metadata are automatically extracted from the text and displayed in the section below, next to their number of instances in the text. Clicking on a metadata will highlight in yellow all its instances and in pink the instance corresponding to the amount of clicks. Clicking on a metadata highlighted in the text will trigger a research in Wikipedia of the corresponding concept.
    We also added a visualization by "Somebody else's problem" to politely ignore things that users sincerely do not care about.
    The different kinds of metadata will be hidden by our reassuring "DON'T PANIC" logo, leaving only the colours to recognize what kind of information will not bother them any longer.
